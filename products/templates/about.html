About Our Cooperative

We are the 1st Provincial Transport Co-op based in Romblon under Registration No. 9520-1018000000046928 issued on September 13, 2019 by the Cooperative Development Authority.

History

ROMBLON TRANSPORT COOPERATIVE (RTC) were created because of the PUV modernization program. The existing local transport operations and van owners joined force to form the cooperative complying with the standards set by law.

For a long time now, land transportation is one of the service problems especially in the province of Romblon. Jeepney/mini buses are age-old thus safety and comforts of passengers are compromised. This is one contributing factor that shows down progress like mobility of goods and passengers are jeopardized.

The ROMBLON TRANSPORT COOPERATIVE (RTC) organized the organization of Tourist, UV Express, Jeepney and Rental transport services in the Province of Romblon and to give comfortable and good services.

The organization of the RTC is aimed to address this problem and is expected to support the development and effort of the government to modernize transport system and ultimately bring about economic development and progress.
